Robotic Sketching

With team of three, created an end-to-end pipeline to sketch an image using an industrial robot arm. I implemented operational space robot control in algorithms C++ and sent commands to KUKA IIWA, and also assisted my teammates in creating a custom end-effector for marker gripping, and computer vision script for image segmentation. Our project code can be found on Github.

CVXCanon

With a team of three, wrote an automatic canonicalization library in C++ for convex optimization problems. Currently used by thousands of researchers and students worldwide as part of CVXPY, where it led to a 6x speedup in solve time.

BioWear

With team of five, designed and built ambulatory biomedical sensors including ICG, PPG, and ECG. Recorded results with Arduino and sent to Ruby on Rails server for user visualization of data. Attempted calculation of higher level biometric statistics, including heartrate and pulse-arrival-time. Won first place in Stanford's Internet of Things Hackathon.

Haptic fMRI Research

Worked under Samir Menon in the Stanford Robotics Lab. Wrote data analysis pipeline to preprocess fMRI data, and use it classify tasks human motions. Broader goal of experiments was to gain greater understanding of human motor control. Co-authored paper, and presented at IEEE's 2014 Engineering in Medicine and Biology Conference.

Warehouse Shelf-Height Optimizer

In team of five, wrote software to help warehouse operators optimize the spacings of their shelvefs to more fully utilize the volume of their warehouse. Project encompassed need-finding, mathematical optimization, and user interface design. Present at judged software fair and won multiple corporate-sponsored awards.

Headphone Equalizer

Built an audio equalizer, controlled by Arduino, that would run a frequency sweep over one pair of headphone, then configure equalizer to make headphones sound like a different, target pair of headphones. Project encompassed analog design, and mathematical optimization techniques. Published an online Instructable, garnering thousands of views.
